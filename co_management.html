Co-Management in Intune

1. What is Co-Management?

Co-Management allows devices to be managed by both ConfigMgr (SCCM) and Intune.

2. Prerequisites

Ensure the following before enabling Co-Management:

  • ConfigMgr (SCCM) 1710 or later.
  • Azure AD Connect for Hybrid Join.
  • Intune and ConfigMgr licenses.

3. Enabling Co-Management

Follow these steps to enable Co-Management:

  • In ConfigMgr, navigate to Administration → Cloud Services → Co-Management.
  • Configure **Workloads** to determine management authority.
  • Ensure compliance policies sync properly.
