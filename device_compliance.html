Device Compliance in Intune

1. Compliance Policies

Ensure devices meet security and management requirements.

Steps:

  • Create a compliance policy in Intune.
  • Define rules such as OS version, encryption, and antivirus status.
  • Assign the policy to users or devices.

2. Conditional Access

Restrict access to corporate resources based on compliance status.

Steps:

  • Configure conditional access policies in Azure AD.
  • Set compliance as a requirement for accessing apps.
  • Apply policies to specific users or groups.

3. Monitoring & Reporting

Track compliance status and take necessary actions.

Steps:

  • View compliance reports in Intune.
  • Identify non-compliant devices and apply remediation.
  • Ensure continuous policy enforcement.
