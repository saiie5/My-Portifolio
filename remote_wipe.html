Remote Wipe in Intune

1. What is Remote Wipe?

Remote wipe allows IT admins to securely erase all data from a device enrolled in Intune.

2. Steps to Perform Remote Wipe

Steps:

  • Sign in to Microsoft Intune Admin Center.
  • Go to Devices â†’ All devices.
  • Select the target device.
  • Click on Wipe and confirm the action.

3. Remote Wipe vs. Retire

Wipe: Completely erases all data and settings from the device.

Retire: Removes company data while keeping personal data intact.
