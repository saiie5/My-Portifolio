Windows Enrollment in Intune

1. Automatic Enrollment

Best for corporate-owned devices that are Azure AD joined.

Steps:

  • Enable MDM auto-enrollment in Azure AD.
  • Assign Intune licenses to users.
  • Users sign in with a work account, and the device enrolls automatically.

2. Windows Autopilot Enrollment

Best for new or reset devices with a zero-touch experience.

Steps:

  • Register devices in Autopilot using hardware IDs.
  • Configure an Autopilot profile in Intune.
  • Assign the profile to devices.
  • Devices connect to the internet and auto-enroll.

3. Manual Enrollment

Best for personal (BYOD) or existing devices.

Steps:

  • Go to Settings → Accounts → Access work or school.
  • Click Connect and sign in with a work account.
  • Device enrolls in Intune and applies policies.
