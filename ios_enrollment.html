iOS Enrollment in Intune

1. Automated Device Enrollment

Best for corporate-owned iOS devices enrolled through Apple Business Manager.

Steps:

  • Ensure your organization is set up with Apple Business Manager.
  • Sync Apple devices with Intune.
  • Assign an enrollment profile to the devices.
  • Devices enroll automatically upon activation.

2. User Enrollment

Best for personal (BYOD) iOS devices where work apps need separation.

Steps:

  • Users download the Microsoft Intune Company Portal app.
  • Sign in with a work account.
  • Follow the guided steps to set up a work profile.
  • Work apps and data are managed separately from personal apps.

3. Device Enrollment

Best for corporate-owned iOS devices where full control is required.

Steps:

  • Reset the device to factory settings if needed.
  • Download and install the Microsoft Intune Company Portal app.
  • Sign in with a work account and follow the enrollment steps.
  • Device enrolls and applies corporate policies automatically.
