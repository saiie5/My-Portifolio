iOS Enrollment in Intune

1. Automated Device Enrollment (ADE)

Best for corporate-owned iOS devices using Apple Business Manager.

Steps:

  • Ensure devices are in Apple Business Manager (ABM).
  • Sync ABM with Intune.
  • Assign devices to an enrollment profile.
  • Devices auto-enroll upon activation.

2. Apple User Enrollment

Best for personal (BYOD) devices with limited management.

Steps:

  • Go to Settings → General → VPN & Device Management.
  • Click Enroll in Intune and follow prompts.
  • Device enrolls with work and personal separation.

3. Manual Enrollment

Best for existing iOS devices without ABM integration.

Steps:

  • Install the **Intune Company Portal** app from the App Store.
  • Sign in with a work account and follow setup instructions.
  • Device enrolls in Intune and applies policies.
