LOB App Deployment in Intune

1. Add LOB App in Intune

LOB (Line-of-Business) apps are internally developed applications for enterprise use.

Steps:

  • Go to Microsoft Intune admin center.
  • Navigate to Apps → Windows → Add.
  • Select Line-of-business app and upload the app package (.msi, .appx, etc.).
  • Configure app details and assignments.
  • Click Save and wait for deployment.

2. Assign LOB App to Devices

Deploy LOB apps to users or devices in your organization.

Steps:

  • Under the app configuration, select Assignments.
  • Choose Required for automatic installation.
  • Select device groups or users to receive the app.
  • Click Save and monitor deployment.
