Android Enrollment in Intune

1. Work Profile Enrollment

Best for BYOD (Bring Your Own Device) scenarios.

Steps:

  • Download and install the Microsoft Intune Company Portal app.
  • Sign in with your work or school account.
  • Follow the on-screen instructions to create a work profile.

2. Fully Managed Enrollment

Best for corporate-owned devices.

Steps:

  • Factory reset the device.
  • During setup, select "Enroll with Microsoft Intune" or scan the QR code provided by IT.
  • Follow on-screen instructions to complete enrollment.

3. Dedicated Device Enrollment

Best for kiosk or shared device scenarios.

Steps:

  • Factory reset the device.
  • Use the enrollment token or QR code provided by Intune.
  • Complete the setup and apply security policies.
