Android Enrollment in Intune

1. Android Enterprise Enrollment

Best for corporate-owned or BYOD Android devices.

Steps:

  • Ensure your organization is set up with Android Enterprise in Intune.
  • Enable Android device enrollment policies.
  • Users sign in with their work account, and the device enrolls automatically.

2. Work Profile Enrollment

Best for personal (BYOD) devices where work apps need separation.

Steps:

  • Users download the Microsoft Intune Company Portal app.
  • Sign in with a work account.
  • Follow the guided steps to set up a work profile.
  • Work apps and data are kept separate from personal apps.

3. Fully Managed Enrollment

Best for corporate-owned devices where full control is required.

Steps:

  • Reset the device to factory settings.
  • On the welcome screen, tap multiple times to begin provisioning.
  • Scan a QR code or enter an enrollment token from Intune.
  • Device enrolls and applies corporate policies automatically.
